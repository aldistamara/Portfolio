Aldis Tamara

Hi! I'm Aldis.

I'm a fresh graduate in Information Systems from Trisakti University with a strong passion for data analysis, ERP systems, system design, and business. Throughout my academic journey, I have gained hands-on experience in Odoo ERP implementation, data visualization using Power BI, and web development with React and Tailwind.

I have actively contributed as a laboratory assistant, industrial teaching assistant, and speaker in ERP training sessions, where I helped students and professionals understand and implement ERP solutions effectively. My research on Transjakarta passenger data forecasting using ARIMA and Power BI has been published in a reputable journal, reflecting my strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Professionally, I have worked as an ERP Odoo Implementation and Web Developer Intern at OK OCE Indonesia, where I successfully implemented various Odoo modules, improved email marketing efficiency, and redesigned the organization’s website. Additionally, as a mentor at PT Anagata Sisedu Nusantara, I taught Scratch-based coding to young students, ensuring a high success rate in their final projects.

With a strong background in system analysis, programming, and business strategy, I am eager to apply my skills in a dynamic environment where I can contribute to innovative technology-driven solutions.
